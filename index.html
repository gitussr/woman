August, Your Month of Supernatural Strength!
Title: Strength Against All Odds

We all need God’s strength; strength to face tough challenges and strength to go on. We need strength to keep going when others lose hope. We need strength to stand and win in the face of opposition and injustice and overwhelming odds. We need strength even though we don't feel like it. We need strength to keep going even when there are odds against us. We need the strength that only God can give. That is why it is called supernatural strength!

The devil has a three-fold mission; to steal, to kill, and to destroy (John 10:10a). He fights to make sure we do not win. He fights to make sure we fail on the verge of success. He fights to make sure we are distracted and discouraged. His fight is to make sure we look at the problems and not the solution. His fight is to make sure we take our eyes away from God; the God of supernatural strength!! But the devil is a liar!! GOD has promised to give us His strength this month; just ask for it daily and keep your eyes on Him.
Click here to read Psalm 18:29-36

https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=psalm+18%3A29-36&version=NASB1995

It was clear that the supernatural strength David possessed came from God. Your strength will come from God also. God was the One who was lifting him, carrying him, empowering him, and exalting him. David said God gave him hind’s feet. The hind is the female deer who is swifter than other deers. It is sure-footed on the side of a mountain, going where others cannot go, escaping what others cannot escape, and leaping over obstacles that are insurmountable. The hind is noted for its agility and unbelievable grace of motion. It has the ability to outrun its enemies. This is what God did for David as he was pursued for years by a relentless enemy, King Saul. According to the above text, we see what God did for David and His will do for you too!

1. God gave him the ability to lead a troop even when he was a fugitive (vs. 29).

2. God was his supernatural shield and protection (vs. 30, 35).

3. God girded him with strength (vs. 32).

4. God made his way blameless (vs. 32).

5. God gave him agility and sure footing. He made his feet like the hind who could go up the side of a cliff with sure footing, all the way to the top to get away from his enemies (vs. 33).

6. God trained his hands to battle (vs. 34).

7. God gave him supernatural strength (vs. 34).

8. God’s right hand held him up (vs. 35).

9. He experienced God’s gentleness even in the time he was in trouble. God helped him and made him great. It is obvious that it was not David’s human ability that made him great, but God’s supernatural power (vs. 35).

10. God enlarged his steps under him and made his feet not to slip. He made his feet like the hind who runs up the side of a mountain. He made small landing spaces became large enough to navigate and he had sure footing where others would have fallen (vs. 36).

I declare and decree that God will strengthen and uphold you. He will be your shield and refuge. He will give you the wisdom to lead wherever you go. You will not be weary. God’s grace will be sufficient for you. God's Spirit of excellence and distinction is your portion. You will not lose your strength. God will back you up. God is training your hands for war and your fingers for battle (Psalm 144:1). You are an arrow in the mighty hand of God. You will not stumble, slip, or fall. You will ride in the wings of the eagle. God will hold you hand by hand and lead you step by step. You will succeed where others fail. You will end well and not fail on the verge of your breakthrough. What God has for you will not be taken by another. Your adversary will not succeed over your life and family. David prevailed over the enemy that pursued him; you will prevail over every enemy of your life and destiny in Jesus’ name.

Number 8 marks a new beginning. I declare this month will be a month of new cycles, new level of mercies, new strength, a new season, and a new era, new victories, and testimonies. God’s mercy will be new every day of this new month and beyond. You are submerged in God’s mercies. I declare the Lord will open a new chapter of your life; a chapter of His supernatural strength and enabling. You will experience His strength in every area of weakness and challenge. Receive strength to do the impossible in Jesus’ name!

Have a victorious new month!
